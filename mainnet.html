Middleman DApp
This is a Nebulas.io decentralized application (DApp).
This DApp operates as a Middleman between a supplier and a consumer. It secure the funds between them during the transfert of goods.
Please select who you are:
Step 1
You can generate a transaction from your wallet to the wallet selected.
You have to wait for the consumer/buyer to generate a transaction for you.
Step 2
You have to wait for the supplier/seller to send you the goods.
You have to send the goods to the consumer/buyer and confirm that send here.
Step 3
When you receive the goods, confirm the reception here.
You have to wait for the consumer/buyer to receive the goods and to confirm it. You'll automatically receive payment when that's done.
